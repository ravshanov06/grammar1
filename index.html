⏳ Present Perfect

Simple: have/has + past participle I have eaten.
Continuous: have/has been + -ing She has been reading.

⏮️ Past Perfect

Simple: had + past participle They had left.
Continuous: had been + -ing He had been waiting.

🔮 Future Perfect

Simple: will have + past participle She will have finished.
Continuous: will have been + -ing I will have been working.
